About ICGL

ICGL is a credit enhancement institution that helps businesses access finance by sharing lending risk with partner financial institutions.

ICGL stands for Imperial Credit Guarantees Limited, reflecting its focus on credit guarantees and financial access support.

Its core mandate is to reduce financing barriers for viable businesses by improving lender confidence through partial guarantees and technical support.

ICGL is generally sector-agnostic and evaluates opportunities based on viability, impact, governance, and alignment with risk criteria.

The institution works with MSMEs and growth-stage businesses that need structured support to access formal financing through partner lenders.

ICGL does not operate like a traditional lender. It supports financing by providing guarantees and advisory support that de-risk lending for financial institutions.

Partial Credit Guarantees

A Partial Credit Guarantee is a risk-sharing instrument that covers a predefined portion of a qualifying loan in the event of borrower default.

Borrowers benefit through improved access to credit, while partner financial institutions benefit from reduced exposure on eligible lending.

Coverage varies by product structure and partner institution, and is typically defined during the guarantee approval process.

Eligibility & Application

Eligible businesses typically apply through partner financial institutions offering lending products supported by ICGL guarantee frameworks.

Criteria often include business registration, financial viability, acceptable governance standards, and compliance with lender underwriting requirements.

Applicants should approach a participating bank or financial institution, which will evaluate the request and determine suitability for ICGL support.

Common requirements include business registration records, management details, financial statements, cashflow projections, and loan purpose documentation.

Foreign-owned businesses may qualify if they meet local regulatory requirements and the participating lender's eligibility conditions.

Timelines depend on documentation quality, credit assessment complexity, and the partner institution's review process.

Partner Financial Institutions (PFIs)

A PFI is a bank or lending institution that works with ICGL to deliver guaranteed financing to eligible businesses.

ICGL collaborates with selected financial institutions whose product strategies and risk frameworks align with its credit enhancement mandate.

Interested institutions can engage ICGL directly for partnership discussions covering underwriting standards, product design, and operational readiness.
